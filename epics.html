EPICS @ VARDHAMAN

EPICS at Vardhaman College of Engineering is a transformative program that empowers engineering students to engage in community service projects, where they can apply their knowledge, skills, and creativity to address real-world challenges faced by communities.

VCE - Engineering Projects in community Services (VISn @ V - Viable Innovative Solutions for community needs at Vardhaman) is one of the best practices in Vardhaman Campus.The programme focuses on identifying the needs of the society and providing a viable and sustainable solution.

Get Started

The primary objectives of EPICS at VCE are:

EPICS at VCE follows a well-structured project implementation process. Students, guided by faculty mentors, identify community needs and challenges. They then conceptualize and propose projects that align with those needs and address them with innovative engineering solutions.


  • Application of Technical Skills

    The program aims to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application. It provides a platform for students to apply their technical skills to design and implement engineering solutions that have a tangible impact on society.

  • Empowering Communities

    EPICS at VCE seeks to empower communities by addressing their needs and challenges. The program focuses on projects that align with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), aiming to improve the quality of life and promote sustainable development in the community..

  • Fostering Innovation

    EPICS encourages students to think innovatively and creatively. By exploring unique engineering solutions, students can develop projects that are effective, efficient, and sustainable.

  • Collaboration and Teamwork

    The program fosters a collaborative approach, emphasizing teamwork and communication skills. Students work in teams to plan, execute, and evaluate projects, learning the importance of cooperation and coordination.


Once the projects are approved, students commence the implementation phase. They engage with community members, understand their perspectives, and work closely with them throughout the project's execution. This approach ensures that the solutions developed are contextually relevant and sustainable.

The impact of EPICS at VCE is twofold - on students and the community:

  • Student Development

    Participating in EPICS fosters personal and professional growth among students. They gain valuable practical experience, leadership skills, and a deeper understanding of societal challenges. The program nurtures a sense of empathy and social consciousness, shaping students into socially responsible engineers.

    • Community Transformation

      EPICS projects have a profound impact on the communities they serve. Whether it's implementing sustainable technologies, improving access to education, or enhancing healthcare facilities, these projects make a positive difference in the lives of community members.
