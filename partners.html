Collaborations and Community Partners

Purdue University

  • Category: University
  • Date of Collaboration: 01 March, 2014
  • Website: engineering.purdue.edu/EPICS

Purdue & Vardhaman

We, at EPICS, Purdue University, and Vardhaman College of Engineering, have joined forces in a groundbreaking collaboration. Led by Professor William Oakes, our partnership focuses on integrating service-learning into the engineering curriculum. Together, we aim to address community needs through innovative projects, fostering cross-cultural experiences for students and making a tangible impact on society.

IEEE in EPICS

  • Category: Non Profit, Technical Organization
  • Date of Collaboration: 01 March, 2018
  • Website: ieee.epics.org

IEEE & Vardhaman

In a dynamic partnership, IEEE and Vardhaman College of Engineering have united their efforts through the EPICS program. Together, we strive to combine IEEE's global expertise with Vardhaman's innovative spirit to tackle pressing community challenges. Through this collaboration, we aim to empower students to apply their engineering skills towards creating sustainable solutions for real-world problems. With a shared commitment to engineering excellence and social responsibility, our collaboration endeavors to make a meaningful impact on communities while nurturing the next generation of socially conscious engineers.

Palle Srujana

  • Category: Non Profit Organization
  • Date of Collaboration: 01 March, 2019
  • Website: pallesrujana.org

Palle Srujana & Vardhaman

In an inspiring collaboration, Palle Srujana, a non-profit organization dedicated to fostering innovation for farmers and agriculture, has partnered with Vardhaman College of Engineering through the EPICS program. Palle Srujana's mission to empower rural communities through innovative solutions finds a natural synergy with Vardhaman's academic environment and engineering expertise. Together, we aim to harness the power of technology and ingenuity to address pressing challenges faced by farmers and agricultural communities. By engaging students in real-world projects focused on agricultural innovation, our collaboration seeks to create sustainable solutions that improve the livelihoods of farmers and contribute to the advancement of agriculture as a whole. Through this partnership, we aspire to instill a sense of social responsibility and entrepreneurship in students while making a meaningful impact on rural communities.

Indo Universal Collaboration for Engineering Education (IUCEE)

  • Category: Educational institution
  • Date of Collaboration: 01 March, 2014
  • Website: iucee.org

IUCEE & Vardhaman

In a groundbreaking initiative, the Indo Universal Collaboration for Engineering Education has joined forces with Vardhaman College of Engineering through the EPICS program. This collaboration represents a fusion of global perspectives and local expertise, aiming to elevate engineering education to new heights of excellence. By leveraging the resources and networks of Indo Universal Collaboration and the innovative spirit of Vardhaman, we seek to empower students to tackle complex engineering challenges with creativity and ingenuity. Through EPICS projects focused on community needs, we aim to nurture a culture of collaboration, problem-solving, and social responsibility among students. Together, we envision a future where engineering education transcends boundaries, fostering a diverse community of engineers committed to making a positive impact on society.
